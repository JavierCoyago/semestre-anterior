Our Adventures

Rafting in Rivers of Ecuador

Get ready for the adventure of a lifetime with rafting in Ecuador's rivers! Navigate through turbulent waters and feel the rush of adrenaline as you tackle thrilling rapids. With breathtaking landscapes and unique biodiversity, each descent is a story you'll tell for years to come.

Rock climbing

Raise your adventurous spirit with rock climbing. Conquer peaks and enjoy panoramic views from the top. Our expert guides will provide you with the instruction and equipment needed to ensure a safe and exhilarating experience, suitable for both beginners and seasoned climbers.

Available Equipment

Kayaks and Canoes

Explore the natural beauty of the rivers with our high-quality kayaks and canoes. Designed for stability and performance, they are perfect for all experience levels. Whether you're looking for a peaceful afternoon of paddling or an exciting white-water adventure, we have the gear you need.

Climbing Equipment

Secure your ascent with our top-of-the-line climbing equipment. From harnesses to helmets and ropes, we provide everything you need for a safe climb. Our gear is certified and maintained to the highest standards, so you can focus on climbing with confidence.
